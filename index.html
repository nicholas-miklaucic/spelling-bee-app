Spelling Bee

How to Play

Try to make words using only the given letters. The center letter must be included in every word. Letters can be repeated and in any order. Each word must also be at least 4 letters long.

Scoring

Scoring is as follows: 4-letter words receive 1 point. Any longer word receives 1 point for each letter, so a 7-letter word receives 7 points. Any word that uses every given letter, called a pangram, receives an additional 7-point bonus.
